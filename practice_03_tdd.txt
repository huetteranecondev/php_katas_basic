# Practice 3 - Test Driven Development
## New Functionality - Arbitrary number of sensors
It shall be possible to have an arbitrary number of sensors that are placed across the building.
The Temperature Controller shall now acquire the temperature from all available sensors and adjust the air condition based on the average temperature of all sensors.

## New Development Practice
Apply [TDD](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Test-driven_development) to implement the new functionality
